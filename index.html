Intake Questionnaire
Answer these questions as if you're in a discovery call with the hiring manager. The more honest and specific, the better your matrix.
Role Basics
Role title & team *
e.g. "Senior Product Designer, Growth team" — be as specific as you know
What problem is this hire solving? *
In plain language — what breaks or doesn't exist without this person?
What does success look like in 90 days?
Concrete outcomes, not vibes. If they say "ramp up" — push for specifics.
What does success look like in 12 months?
Context & Constraints
Seniority level
Company stage
Who does this person report to & collaborate with daily?
What existing tools, stack, or systems must they already know?
Are there any hard dealbreakers? (background, geography, sector, compliance, etc.)
Culture & Working Style
What traits does the hiring manager mention unprompted?
What do they keep coming back to? Ownership? Speed? Communication? Rigor?
Who is a good internal analogy for this role?
Think of someone at the company who would succeed in this role — what makes them good?
What type of candidates have NOT worked out in similar roles here?
Past failures tell you as much as past successes.
Anything else the hiring manager said that felt important?
